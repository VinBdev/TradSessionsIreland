    • Number 2: Guitar
    The guitar is a wonderful string instrument that is the heart and soul of most musical groups or as a solo instrument. This instrument can also be used as a percussion instrument and is a central instrument for trad sessions. The guitar often will help other musicians stay on time for the song being played, so various players can listen to the timing of the guitar and give a good indicatoin of when to play their instruments at the right time. Individually guitar can be used for most genres of music and is always a good one to have at a party for sings songs as well.
